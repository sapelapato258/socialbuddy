Our Story

Built for Students,
By Students

SocialBuddy was born from a simple idea — every student deserves access to quality academic support, without the stress.

Our Mission

SocialBuddy is a student-first academic support platform designed to bridge the gap between struggling and excelling. We provide everything from handwritten notes and assignment help to online coaching and study resources.

Founded in Mysuru, we started as a small peer-support group and grew into a full-fledged platform serving thousands of students across Karnataka and beyond.

We believe that every student — regardless of their background or resources — deserves a fair chance at academic success.
